Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is the ability to read the emotional environment within and around us, this is the feelings and emotions. For example, if we feel upset, by noticing that we are experiencing this feeling, we can control how we react rather than unconsciously reacting. This uncouncsious reaction can negatively affect the environement around us and disturb our inner calm. By noticing our emotion and feelings, we are making a choice in how to deal with this emotion. It is what some would say “street smart”.

How is this different from IQ? Well, IQ is a measure of academic ability, to analyse information and use logic, remember, recall and apply information or what is called “book smart".

We live in a place where change is constant and complex.
IQ matters in helping navigate this ever changing place but not as much as EQ.
Having the ability to regulate ourselves and read the emotions of others will lead to better relationships. It is am important factor in good leadership and personal performance and team performance and overall happiness.
Understanding the emotions within our selves and others around us and guiding our actions accordingly, WE ARE IN CONTROL.

Luckily, this valuable skill can be learnt.
